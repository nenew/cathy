公司介绍

　　浙江凯西国际货运代理有限公司是经浙江省商务部批准的一级国际货运代理企业，备案编号（00042015）。注册资本人民币伍佰零壹万元。本公司专业经营国际运输业务，向海内外客户提供海、陆、空进出口货物运输服务，包括订舱、配载、报关、仓储、提运、保险以及多项物流服务，业务涵盖：普货、一般化工品、危险品、大型工程项目及大型货物运输等。

　　本公司拥有自己的内装仓库和集装箱、散货车队并拥有一支经验丰富，业务娴熟，具备良好职业操守的员工队伍，能竭诚为广大新老客户提供安全、迅速、准确、低廉、便捷的国际货运服务。

　　无论何时，凯西国际货运代理都以“团结奋进，努力拼搏”之企业精神与“诚信服务、顾客至上、不断进取”的经营理念作为企业永恒的服务宗旨，向更高的标准而努力，希望广大客户与凯西国际货运代理携手共同开拓新的市场。

Company profile

　　Zhejiang Cathy international freight agency co., LTD is approved by the ministry of commerce of zhejiang province first-level international freight forwarding enterprise, for the record number (00042015). Zero total registered capital of RMB ten thousand yuan. Our company specializing in international shipping business, In mainland of China and abroad to provide land, sea and air import and export cargo transportation services, including booking, loading, customs clearance, warehousing, delivery, insurance, and a number of logistics service, the business covers: general cargo, general chemicals, dangerous goods, large projects and large cargo transport, etc.

　　Within the company we have our own warehouse and container, bulk cargo fleet and has an experienced, skilled business, have good professional personal integrity of the staff, will wholeheartedly for the general new and old customers to provide safe, rapid, accurate, cheap and convenient international shipping service.

　　Whenever, Cathy international freight forwarder with "unity and hard work" spirit of enterprise and the "integrity services, customer first, continuous improvement" business philosophy as the enterprise eternal service purpose, to a higher standard, hope the broad masses of customers hand in hand with Kathy international freight agency jointly develop new markets.
